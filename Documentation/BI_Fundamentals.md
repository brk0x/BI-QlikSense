# Business Intelligence Fundamentals

## What is BI?

Business Intelligence (BI) is the process of transforming raw data into meaningful insights for decision-making.

## Key Components

- Data Sources
- Data Integration (ETL/ELT)
- Data Warehouse
- Data Modeling
- Reporting & Visualization

## BI Goals

- Improve decision-making
- Increase operational efficiency
- Enable data-driven culture
